Product Usage Process

Each roof is inspected first. Product choices depend on the existing roof type, condition, adhesion, weather, manufacturer guidance, and whether the project is eligible for up to a 20-year warranty.

Inspect

Identify substrate, leaks, rust, seams, ponding areas, penetrations, and whether restoration is appropriate.

  • Document roof type
  • Find repair scope
  • Confirm product path

Prepare

Clean and prepare the roof so primer, fabric, sealants, and coatings have a sound surface.

  • Cleaner as needed
  • Rust treatment as needed
  • Primer when required

Detail

Reinforce weak points before field coating so common leak areas are handled first.

  • Seams
  • Fasteners
  • Penetrations and transitions

Coat & Verify

Apply the selected Conklin system, check coverage, and review the finished waterproofing and reflective surface.

  • Base/top coat as specified
  • Wet film checks
  • Warranty path reviewed when eligible
